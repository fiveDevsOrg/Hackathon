# Rules & Format

## The one rule

Build something **5 people would pay for**, and come to demo day with **evidence they
will.** Ranked by `MONEY-VALIDATION.md` levels. Real money beats everything.

## Who

5 builders, each shipping their **own** MVP in their **own** repo, with their **own** stack.
Shared standards only — no shared codebase.

## Timeline (1 week)

| Day | Focus |
|----:|-------|
| **Day 0 — Kickoff** | Claim an idea (no dupes), set your validation target, create your repo from the template. |
| **Days 1–2 — Build** | ~48h core build. Get a live URL + a working money mechanism up ASAP — even before the product is "done." |
| **Days 3–5 — Distribute** | Drive real traffic. Post, DM, cold-email, list on a marketplace. Chase the funnel, not features. |
| **Day 6 — Demo day** | Show live URL + funnel numbers. Ranked live. |

> Adjust the calendar to your group — the shape (short build, longer distribution) is the
> point. Money proof needs traffic, and traffic takes more than a weekend.

## Daily standup

One comment per day in the `hackathon` repo (Discussions or a pinned Issue):
```
@handle — Day N
Shipped: <what went live>
Funnel: <visits / clicks / signups / $>
Blocker: <one thing>
```

## Demo day format (5–7 min each)

1. The one-sentence pitch (who/problem/what they pay for).
2. Live URL — show the landing page + the money mechanism working.
3. The funnel numbers. Visits → clicks → signups → dollars.
4. Your validation level + whether you hit your target.

## Judging / win condition

Ranked **only** by validation level (see `MONEY-VALIDATION.md`):

1. **Level 3 — real money collected** wins. Tie-break: most revenue.
2. **Level 2 — pre-orders / LOIs.** Tie-break: total committed $.
3. **Level 1 — waitlist signups.** Tie-break: signups, then % from cold traffic.
4. **Level 0 — live + analytics.** Participation.

No points for: prettiest UI, most code, cleverest model usage. Those help you *sell* —
they are not the score.

## Honesty rules

- Report real numbers. Inflated funnels help no one and are obvious on demo day.
- Manual delivery (concierge MVP) is allowed and encouraged — just disclose it.
- "Friends-and-family" signups count, but flag what % came from cold traffic.

## Definition of done (per MVP)

Live public URL · pricing with a real number · working money mechanism · a tracked funnel
with real numbers · README filled from `PROJECT-TEMPLATE.md` · a stated validation level.

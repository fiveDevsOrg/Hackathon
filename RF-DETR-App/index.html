Gesture Controls

Start the camera, keep your hands visible, then use these gestures to control the sandbox.

Pinch hold

Touch thumb and index finger near the selected shape, then move your hand to drag it.

Two open hands

Move both open hands apart or together to zoom the selected shape.

Open-hand swipe

Swipe left or right with one open hand to switch between the square, circle, and triangle.
